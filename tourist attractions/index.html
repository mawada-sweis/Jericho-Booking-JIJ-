Don't know where to go? Learn about the tour guide

Why should we walk with a tour guide?

Guides are known to be cultural ambassadors to their country and community. They provide safe, enjoyable and fun travel experiences for their guest. Priding themselves in being able to perfectly blend the professionalsim needed for the job with the hospitality and welcoming spirit of friendship that makes the guest feel like they have spent the day with a close friend. So is it important for a guide to belong to an association or should they just be independent.




How can I choose the tour guide?

Here are qualities a great tour guide should possess:
- Knowledge of the Tour Area.
- Ability to Communicate Effectively.
- Strong Empathy and Understanding.
- Charismatic Personality.
- Keen Ability to Improvise and Adapt.
- Engaging Storyteller and Actor.
- Flexibility.
