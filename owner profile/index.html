Facilities

Jericho Resort Village has 58 hotel rooms, 46 bungalows and two studio-suites. All rooms have private facilities, individual climate control, telephone, radio, satellite TV, and a mini-bar. The bungalows have terraces or balconies overlooking the pool area, a living room with a couch, kitchenette, a double bedroom, and all the amenities of the hotel rooms. Jericho Resort Village offers lots of facilities such as swimming pools for adults and kids, Jacuzzis, multipurpose playing grounds (tennis, basketball, handball), sand volleyball, children’s playgrounds and occasional horse and camel activities.
