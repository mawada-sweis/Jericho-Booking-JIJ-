Why Jericho Resort Village?

Jericho Resort Village is one of the best choices for relaxation if you want to get away from the noise of mega-cities. Jericho Resort Village provides trips to Ein Gedi and Massada, St. George's Monastery in Wadi Quelt and Hisham Palace. Jericho Resort Village is very close to Jericho’s famous cable car, which takes you to the top of Mount of Temptation to find a beautiful restaurant with a great view of the city.
